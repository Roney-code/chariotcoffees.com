OUR JOURNEY

Who We Are

Was first registered in Uganda in 2018 as (A Single Member Company SMC) under the Directorship of Mr. Simon Ochen Eidodo.But later in 2024 it acquired a new status as a private limited company upon admitting Mr. Gilbert Etomet as a Co- Director.

Today, the company amongst its major goals, directly impacts lives through its academy which focuses on training young people into becoming seasonal Coffee Baristas and in recent years over 2000 young men and women have passed through the Academy successfully and are now spread all over the world excelling as Baristas amongst others.

OUR EXPERTISE

What We Offer

Specialty Coffee

Single-origin and custom blends roasted in-house to perfection. Experience the full spectrum of coffee flavors from Africa's finest growing regions.

Barista Training

Comprehensive courses from beginner to professional level. Learn latte art, espresso extraction, and coffee science from certified trainers.

Wholesale Supply

Premium coffee beans and equipment for businesses. Custom roasting profiles tailored to your establishment's needs.

Café Experience

Artisanal beverages and a welcoming space to work or relax. Seasonal specials featuring innovative brewing methods.

Sustainability

Ethical sourcing and eco-friendly practices. We partner directly with farmers to ensure fair compensation and quality.

Events

Cupping sessions, workshops, and community events. Join our monthly coffee enthusiast meetups and tastings.

OUR FUTURE

Vision & Values

Vision Statement

To be Africa's leading coffee academy and roastery, where knowledge meets passion and every cup tells a story. We aim to raise the bar of quality, ethics, and innovation in the coffee industry.

Core Values

  • Quality: Uncompromising standards in every bean and brew
  • Education: Empowering through knowledge and skills
  • Sustainability: Ethical sourcing and environmental responsibility
  • Community: Building connections through coffee culture
